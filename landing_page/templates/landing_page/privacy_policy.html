What data do we collect?

CodePlay collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email address
  • Your education level
  • Your current place of employment/education
  • The questions you attempt and the ones you solve
  • The time it took for you to solve any question

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide CodePlay with all of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register online on our website.
  • Voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback on any of our message boards or via email.
  • Use or view our website via your browser's cookies.
  • Attempt or complete a coding question on our website

How will we use your data ?

CodePlay collects your data so that we can:

  • Show how fast you can solve problems compared to other people
  • Allow you to automatically log in when you visit the website again

How do we store your data?

CodePlay securely stores your data in Sweden, the security precautions taken are the standard ones undertaken by AWS. More information can be found here

CodePlay will keep all the data you have given, until the 1st of June, 2024. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by removing all your records from the website's database.

What are your data protection rights?

CodePlay would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access
You have the right to request CodePlay for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to access
You have the right to request CodePlay for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification
You have the right to request that CodePlay correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request CodePlay to complete information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure
You have the right to request that CodePlay erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing
You have the right to request CodePlay restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing
You have the right to object to CodePlay's processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability
You have the right to request that CodePlay transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: AXS2175@student.bham.ac.uk or call us at: +44 (0)121 414 3344

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behaviour information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

CodePlay uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including keeping you signed in.

What type of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses

  • Functionality - CodePlay uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The CodePlay website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

CodePlay keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 6th March 2024.
